About Me

With 3 years of hands-on experience in UX and product design, I specialize in transforming complex problems into intuitive, user-friendly solutions. My approach is rooted in user research and usability testing, ensuring that every design decision is backed by real user insights.

I excel at bridging the gap between design and development. Working closely with developers throughout my career has given me a unique perspective on design-to-development collaboration. I translate my designs into responsive interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allows me to prototype quickly and communicate effectively with engineering teams.

My toolkit includes Figma for design and prototyping, FigJam for collaborative workshops, and various usability testing tools to validate design decisions. I leverage AI-assisted workflows to enhance productivity and explore innovative solutions. This combination of design thinking, technical skills, and problem-solving enables me to deliver end-to-end digital experiences that users love.
