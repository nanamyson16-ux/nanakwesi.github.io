Overview

Project Summary

FlyEase is a flight booking app focused on simplifying a process that often feels overwhelming and stressful for users. From searching flights to completing a booking, the goal was to design an experience that feels clear, efficient, and trustworthy.

Goal

Design a flight booking experience that feels clear, efficient, and trustworthy from search to checkout.

Target Users

Frequent and occasional travelers who find flight booking apps stressful or confusing.

Platform

Mobile App — iOS-first design with Android considerations for a global audience.

Challenges

Key Design Challenges

Three core tensions shaped every design decision throughout this project.

01

Cognitive Overload

Users face too many decisions at once — flight times, fares, seats, baggage, and extras all competing for attention simultaneously.

02

Building Trust

Users need confidence in pricing and payments. Hidden fees and ambiguous costs erode trust before checkout is even reached.

03

Speed vs. Clarity

Balancing fast booking without confusing the user — a streamlined flow that doesn't sacrifice the information users need.

User Research

Research Summary

User feedback and analytics revealed clear opportunities to improve usability, efficiency, and overall satisfaction across the booking journey.

User Interviews
Usability Testing
Analytics Review

Complex Navigation

Users found it challenging to navigate the app, especially when searching for specific flights or modifying travel details mid-flow.

Information Overload

The abundance of information during booking overwhelmed users. They struggled to filter and prioritize flight times, prices, and amenities.

Transparency Issues

Users expressed concerns about hidden fees and ambiguous pricing structures. This eroded trust and negatively affected the overall experience.

Research Conclusion

What the Research Revealed

Three clear opportunity areas emerged from analyzing existing booking patterns and common user frustrations.

Clearer Navigation Structure

Core actions like flight search, filters, and booking details were scattered — making the experience feel unintuitive. A restructured navigation was needed.

Step-by-Step Booking Flow

The booking flow felt heavier than necessary. A clear, step-by-step process with visible progress indicators would reduce uncertainty at each stage.

Pricing Transparency

Hidden fees were a key trust issue. Clearer cost breakdowns and a pre-payment summary page let users review the full cost before committing.

Design

Design Decisions

With a clear understanding of user goals and pain points, the designs focus on creating a flight booking experience that is intuitive, efficient, and trustworthy.

Feature 01

Simplified Navigation

Users struggled to navigate the app and find or modify flight details. The redesign focuses on a clear, structured menu that groups search, filters, and booking details — with quick-access shortcuts for modifying travel information. This ensures users can find what they need quickly and without confusion.

Feature 02

Streamlined Information

Booking was overwhelming due to too much information at once. The redesign breaks the process into a step-by-step flow with visible progress indicators, while grouping essential details such as flight times, fares, and baggage options. This helps users focus on the most important information at each stage.

Feature 03

Transparent Pricing

Users were concerned about hidden fees and unclear costs. The redesign highlights all charges upfront and introduces a pre-payment summary page with a complete cost breakdown before checkout — improving trust and confidence before the user commits.

Design Validation

How Decisions Were Validated

Design decisions were validated through iterative reviews and established UX best practices applied to flight booking experiences.

01

Reduced Cognitive Load

Navigation structure and booking flow were refined using usability principles focused on reducing the number of decisions users face at any one moment.

02

System Status Visibility

Progress indicators and step-by-step flows ensure users always know where they are in the booking journey, minimising uncertainty and drop-off.

03

Pricing Transparency

The pre-payment summary page was validated as an effective trust-building mechanism before the user commits to checkout.

Takeaways

Reflections

What this project reinforced about designing for clarity and trust.

Working on FlyEase reminded me how overwhelming flight booking can feel when things aren't clear.

This project encouraged me to focus on structure, clarity, and transparency — designing screens that help users move forward with confidence rather than hesitation.

Redesigning an existing product sharpened my ability to identify friction quickly and transform complex flows into simple, usable experiences.

Structure and transparency are not just design principles — they are trust-building tools, especially when users are making financial decisions on the go.

Every screen should answer the user's implicit question: "What do I do next?" — removing hesitation at each step is the real measure of success.
